      **üöÄ Instant Extraction for CSV/TSV/PSV Files**

      We're excited to introduce a significant enhancement to our import process. With the removal of the extraction step for CSV/TSV/PSV files, the import experience is now more seamless than ever. As soon as the upload is complete, these files are instantly extracted, making for an efficient and immediate handling of your data.

      The impact of this change is remarkable. **What used to take approximately 3 and a half minutes to extract now concludes in less than 10 seconds.**

      The star of this enhancement is the new DuckDB storage engine. While speed is its prime advantage, there's a broader positive effect. This update simplifies and enhances reliability in our system. Previously, concerns about only a fraction of a file being extracted are now history. Furthermore, this approach strategically eases the load on our Postgres database, reducing the likelihood of encountering resource limits.

      In essence, this upgrade doesn't just boost performance; it elevates the import process with improved speed, simplicity, reliability, and resource management. It's a win-win for both efficiency and user experience.
